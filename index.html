Wedding FAQs

What Is the Dress Code?

We're keeping the event super casual, so please feel free to let your freak flag fly! However, we will be instituting a strict theme of "Natural Earth Goodness". If you do not comply with the theme - that's ok. All we ask is that you help maintain our desired wedding aesthetic by keeping a wide berth of our wedding documentarians.

What Happens After the Ceremony?

After the binding ceremony, we will be moving festivities to the Amityville marsh! Music will be provided by "The Better-Than-You's" our favourite local jug/raga band. There will also be a selection of nibbles provided (although we do encourage you to bring your own main course). And a wedding wouldn't be a wedding without delicious beverages, so we will be serving our famous homemade 3% kombucha for adults as well as non-alcoholic keffir for the kids!

Will the Venue Offer Free Parking?

In the interest of respecting Mother Earth, we ask that guests do not take combustion-engine vehicles to our special day. if you insist on sprewing toxic poison into the atmosphere, then maybe you would be better off not coming at all. Those riding bikes, may want to bring some wooden planks with them on the day to ensure that their ride doesn't sink into the marsh. x
